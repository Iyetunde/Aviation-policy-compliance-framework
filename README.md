# Aviation Policy Compliance Framework

Designed a risk based compliance framework for an aviation environment, focused on turning policies into enforceable controls aligned with ISO standards.

## Project Overview

This project presents a structured Policy Compliance Framework developed to address gaps in policy enforcement, monitoring, and accountability within an aviation setting.

The framework focuses on moving beyond documentation to practical implementation by introducing a risk driven approach to compliance.

## Key Areas Covered

- Information Security Governance  
- Identity and Access Management  
- Incident Management  
- Secure Configuration  
- Data Protection and Privacy  
- Artificial Intelligence Governance  
- Third Party Risk Management  

## Standards Alignment

- ISO/IEC 27002  
- ISO/IEC 42001  

## Included Documents

- Policy Compliance Framework (Presentation)  
- Artificial Intelligence Policy  
- Data Protection and Privacy Policy  

## Objective

To demonstrate how a well structured compliance framework can improve regulatory readiness, reduce operational risk, and strengthen governance in a regulated industry.

## Key Insight

Policies alone do not create compliance.  
Enforcement, monitoring, and accountability do.
